What happens after you upload

Preprocessing — the image is resized, normalized and staged in Amazon S3 for inference.
Inference — a ResNet18 classifier hosted on an AWS SageMaker endpoint scores the image.
Explainability — Grad‑CAM generates an attention heatmap over the region that influenced the result.
Decision — the item is classified as Normal or Defective with a confidence score.
